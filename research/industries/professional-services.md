## Locations

| Type | Trigger | Monthly Cost | Effect |
|---|---|---|---|
| Solo Practitioner Office | Founding (1 attorney/consultant/CPA) | $1,500–$3,500 | Baseline capacity: 1 biller; enables IOLTA account, bar membership |
| Shared / Co-working Office | Founding – 3 billers | $800–$2,000 | Lower overhead; no dedicated conference room; limits client perception |

| Primary Office (Mid-Size) | 10–25 billers OR $2M ARR | $10,000–$25,000 | Enables full admin department, library, reception; flagship location |
| Full-Floor Headquarters | 30+ billers OR $8M ARR | $25,000–$60,000 | Prestige signal; supports 50+ staff; enables C-suite hires (COO, CFO) |
| Satellite Office (Same Metro) | 15+ billers at HQ + regional client demand | $5,000–$12,000 | Overflow capacity; geographic coverage; leverages existing brand |
| Second City Office | $5M+ ARR OR 3+ equity partners | $12,000–$35,000 | New geographic market; requires local bar admission (law) or CPA license reciprocity; own P&L |
| Virtual / Registered Office | Regulatory compliance in new state | $200–$800 | Satisfies state bar foreign qualification or CPA firm registration; no headcount |
| International Office | $25M+ ARR OR anchor client in market | $20,000–$80,000 | Cross-border capability; local bar/regulatory compliance required; local partner hire mandatory in many jurisdictions |

| Deposition / Hearing Suite | Litigation practice 10+ cases active | $1,500–$4,000/month | Reduces vendor rental costs; enables in-house videography and court reporter hosting |
| Conference Center Annex | 25+ billers; frequent large client meetings | $5,000–$15,000 | Client events, pitch presentations, partner retreats; BD multiplier |

## Revenue Streams

| Type | Trigger | Monthly Cost | Effect |
|---|---|---|---|
| Billable Hours (Hourly Rate) | Firm founding | $0 (revenue generator) | Core model; rate $150–$2,100/hr depending on seniority and market; most common; 100% of firms use at some stage |
| Retainer / Monthly Fixed Access Fee | Established client relationship | $0 | Predictable ARR; consulting retainers $2,000–$10,000/client/month; general counsel retainers $3,000–$15,000/month |

| Success / Contingency Component (Consulting) | M&A advisory, performance improvement | $0 | Fee tied to deal close or measurable outcome; typically 0.5–2% of transaction value |
| Value-Based / Outcome Fee | Sophisticated clients; quantifiable deliverable | $0 | Fee tied to measurable value delivered; highest margin potential |

## Industry-Specific Metrics

- **Utilization Rate**: Percentage of workday applied to billable work. Industry average 38% (Clio 2025); 68.9% of targeted billable hours actually billed (Deltek 2025). Target: 75–80% of budgeted hours.
- **Realization Rate**: Percentage of billable work actually invoiced (after discounts/write-downs). Industry median ~88% (Clio 2025); Am Law 100 average declining to ~81% (2024).
- **Collection Rate**: Percentage of invoiced work actually paid. Industry average 93% (Clio 2025). Target 95%+.
- **Billing Rate (Rack Rate)**: Published hourly rate by role. Am Law 50 senior partners: $2,100/hr avg (2025). Solo/small firm associates: $150–$350/hr.
- **Leverage Ratio**: Associates + non-partner billers per equity partner. Optimal: 4:1. Big 4: 10–20:1. High leverage = more scalable profit per partner.
- **Profit Per Equity Partner (PPP)**: Primary profitability metric. Am Law 100 average: $3.15M (2024), up 12.3% YoY.
- **Revenue Per Lawyer (RPL)**: Total firm revenue divided by all lawyers. Key efficiency indicator.
- **Lockup Days**: Days of revenue tied up in unbilled work + unpaid invoices. Industry median 93 days total.
- **Origination Credit**: Revenue attributed to the partner who brought in the client. Drives compensation.
- **Matter Profitability**: Revenue per matter minus direct costs minus allocated overhead.
- **Billable Hours Budget**: Annual target per biller. Associates 1,800–2,200 hrs/yr (BigLaw); partners 1,200–1,600.
- **Write-Off Rate**: Percentage of billed time reduced or eliminated before invoicing. 72% of firms saw write-offs increase recently.
- **Effective Billing Rate (EBR)**: Actual revenue collected / hours worked = standard rate x realization x collection. True monetization measure.
- **Engagement Margin**: Matter revenue minus direct labor cost divided by revenue. Target 40–60%.
- **Client Concentration Risk**: Revenue from top 1/5/10 clients as % of total. Single client >20% is high-risk.

## Growth Stages

| Stage | Threshold | What Unlocks |
|---|---|---|
| Solo Practitioner | 1 biller; $0–$300K ARR | IOLTA trust account; malpractice insurance; bar membership; shared office; bookkeeper; basic billing software |
| Emerging Firm | 2–4 billers; $300K–$750K ARR | First associate; paralegal; dedicated leased office; legal secretary; referral partner network; basic website |
| Small Firm / Boutique | 5–10 billers; $750K–$2M ARR | Second equity partner; office manager; billing coordinator; first practice group identity; Westlaw subscription |
| Growing Firm | 10–20 billers; $2M–$5M ARR | HR manager; recruiting coordinator; marketing manager; conflicts/risk manager; CRM; second office; non-equity partner tier |
| Mid-Size Firm | 20–50 billers; $5M–$15M ARR | Finance controller; IT department; legal tech specialist; knowledge management; BD director; practice groups (3+); Chambers submission |
| Regional Firm | 50–100 billers; $15M–$40M ARR | CFO; COO; CHRO; DEI director; CISO; full billing department; summer associate class; multiple offices; government contracts |
| National Firm | 100–300 billers; $40M–$150M ARR | General counsel (internal); managing partner structure; practice group BD managers; national CLE program; alumni network; Am Law 200 ranking |
| AmLaw 100 / Big 4 Tier | 300+ billers; $150M+ ARR | International offices; C-suite fully staffed; equity/non-equity two-tier; internal analytics team; innovation/AI team; Chambers Band 1 target |
