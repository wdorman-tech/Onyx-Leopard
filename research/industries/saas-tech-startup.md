## Industry-Specific Metrics

- **MRR / ARR**: Monthly/Annual Recurring Revenue. Primary health indicator. All stage thresholds reference this.
- **MRR Growth Rate (MoM)**: Top-decile: 10–17% early; 6–7% post $3M ARR. Below 5% at seed is warning.
- **Gross Revenue Retention (GRR)**: Revenue retained excluding expansion. Healthy B2B: >85%. Enterprise: >90%.
- **Net Revenue Retention (NRR/NDR)**: Revenue retained plus expansion minus churn. Best-in-class: >120%. Median: ~106%.
- **Customer Churn Rate**: Healthy: <5%/yr enterprise; <15%/yr SMB. Monthly gross >2% is red flag.
- **CAC**: Total S&M spend / new customers. Median SaaS: ~$702 SMB; enterprise $50K+.
- **LTV**: ARPU / churn rate. LTV:CAC of 3:1 minimum; 5:1+ best-in-class.
- **CAC Payback Period**: Months to recover CAC from gross margin. Median 15–18 months; elite <12.
- **ARPU**: Total MRR / paying customers. Rising = successful up-market motion.
- **Pipeline Coverage**: Qualified pipeline / quarterly target. 3–4x standard; <2x = quarter at risk.
- **Sales Cycle Length**: SMB 14–30 days; Mid-market 45–90; Enterprise 90–270+.
- **Win Rate**: Qualified opps closed-won. Healthy: 20–35%. Below 15% = issues.
- **Magic Number**: Net new ARR / prior quarter S&M. Above 0.75 = efficient; >1.0 exceptional.
- **Rule of 40**: Growth rate (%) + EBITDA margin (%). Above 40 = healthy balance.
- **Gross Margin**: Revenue - COGS. Healthy pure-software: 70–85%. Below 60% = concern.
- **Burn Multiple**: Net cash burned / net new ARR. Efficient: <1.5x. Above 2x = concern.
- **ARR per Employee**: Series A: $20–25K. Series C: $50–66K. Top public: $200K+.
- **NPS**: B2B SaaS benchmark: >30 good; >50 excellent.
- **DAU/MAU Ratio**: Above 20% = habitual product; below 10% = churn risk.
- **Expansion MRR Rate**: Target >20% of total new MRR from expansion at Series B+.
- **Quota Attainment**: Healthy team: 55–70%. Below 40% = quota-setting or PMF issues.
- **Months of Runway**: Cash / monthly net burn. Minimum 18 months; 24+ preferred.

## Growth Stages

| Stage | Threshold | What Unlocks |
|---|---|---|
| 0 — Pre-Product | 0 employees; 0 revenue | Founding team; home/incubator; advisory board; legal; angel conversations |
| 1 — MVP / PSF | 1–5 employees; $0–$10K MRR | First engineer; AWS; GitHub; auth; beta users |
| 2 — Early Traction | 5–15 employees; $10K–$100K MRR | Coworking suite; first AE; UX designer; HubSpot; Stripe; seed round |
| 3 — PMF Confirmed | 15–30 employees; $100K–$500K MRR | Leased HQ; SDR team; PMM; content/SEO; Series A; DevOps; SOC 2; VP Sales |
| 4 — Scale-Up | 30–75 employees; $500K–$2M MRR | Enterprise AE; SE; CMO; Head of Eng; FP&A; RevOps; Series B; secondary region; AWS Marketplace |
| 5 — Growth | 75–200 employees; $2M–$8M MRR | CRO; CFO; Head of People; ML/AI; data warehouse; legal; compliance; intl offices; Series C; analyst relations |
| 6 — Enterprise Scale | 200–500 employees; $8M–$25M MRR | VP People; TAMs; marketplace; multi-cloud; SI/channel partners; FedRAMP/HIPAA; growth equity |
| 7 — Pre-IPO / Mature | 500+ employees; $25M+ MRR | Public readiness (SOX prep); M&A; global offices; data product; Rule of 40 optimization; IPO |
