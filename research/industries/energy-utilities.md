Primary growth unlock metric.\n- MW Capacity — Pipeline: Total MW in active development (site control through NTP). Signals near-term growth.\n- Capacity Factor (%): Actual MWh generated / theoretical max MWh. Solar: 21–34%; Onshore wind: 30–45%; CCGT: 50–60%; Geothermal: 80–95%.\n- Net Capacity Factor (%): Capacity factor adjusted for planned and forced outages.\n- Availability Factor (%): Hours available for dispatch / total hours. Best-in-class solar 98–99%; wind 95–97%.\n- Forced Outage Rate (%): Unplanned outages as % of available hours. Directly impacts PPA performance guarantees.\n- Curtailment Rate (%): MWh curtailed / total potential MWh. High curtailment indicates interconnection or transmission constraint.\n\n**Economics**\n- LCOE ($/MWh): Levelized Cost of Energy. Utility solar 2024: $24–$40/MWh; Onshore wind: $25–$50/MWh; Offshore wind: $80–$130/MWh; CCGT: $40–$70/MWh; Geothermal: $60–$100/MWh.\n- Heat Rate (BTU/kWh): Thermal plant efficiency metric. CCGT best-in-class: 6,000–6,400 BTU/kWh. Higher = less efficient = higher fuel cost.\n- Project IRR (%): Unlevered and levered project returns. Unlevered 6–9% typical for contracted renewables; levered 10–15% with tax equity.\n- Development Pipeline Conversion Rate (%): MW reaching NTP / MW entering pipeline. Industry average 10–20% of GW-scale pipeline.\n\n**Interconnection and Grid**\n- Grid Interconnection Queue Position: Active position in ISO/RTO cluster study. Earlier positions have lower network upgrade cost allocations.\n- PPA Contract Backlog (MW): Total MW under executed PPAs not yet at COD. Key investor metric for revenue visibility.\n- PPA Contract Backlog ($/MWh weighted average): Blended contracted price across portfolio. Indicates portfolio quality.\n- Transmission Network Upgrade Obligation ($): Assigned cost share from interconnection cluster study. Must be funded at financial close.\n\n**Storage and Flexibility**\n- Round-trip Efficiency (%): BESS discharge MWh / charge MWh. Li-ion 85–92%.\n- Duration (Hours): MWh capacity / MW power rating. 2-hr, 4-hr, 8-hr, 12-hr; longer duration unlocks capacity markets.\n- Cycles Per Year: Annual charge-discharge cycles. Affects battery degradation and warranty.\n\n**Financial**\n- Tax Equity Utilization ($): Total ITC/PTC credits monetized to date. Scales with operating fleet; unlocks complex tax structures.\n- Debt Service Coverage Ratio (DSCR): Operating cashflow / debt service. Minimum 1.25x typical lender covenant.\n- Project-Level Revenue Per MW ($/MW/mo): Blended across PPA, merchant, capacity, and ancillary.\n\n**Reliability (Regulated Utility Perspective)**\n- SAIDI (min/customer/yr): System Average Interruption Duration Index. Regulated targets typically < 60–90 minutes.\n- SAIFI (interruptions/customer/yr): System Average Interruption Frequency Index. Regulated target < 1.0–1.5.\n- Carbon Intensity (tonnes CO2/MWh): Portfolio-wide emissions rate. IRA compliance and ESG reporting.\n\n---\n\n## Growth Stages\n\n| Stage | Threshold | What Unlocks |\n|---|---|---|\n| 1 — Pre-Development | Founding; 0 MW operating; < $1M raised | Project development office;
